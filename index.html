spice n bhas is a state of art e-store aiming at providing a wide variety of freshly prepared ingredients that are not only essential to make your food with love and taste but also have anti microbial properties. We collected it directly from farmers and growers, sorted , cleaned and couriered to our customers without the help any middleman. thats why we are giving lowest price to all essentials. The farms are located at varied regions of kerala most suited for the cultivation of spice and fruits. Our products are distinctive in providing the natural color, taste, flavour, aroma and texture and also calorie free to enhance and make food delicious and to boost your metabolism and improve your health at the same time. You will get a wide variety of all spices and fruits from spice n bhas which are packed in the finest way to keep up their freshness. spice n bhas is mainly focusing on whole-sale exporting of spices all over india and abroad.
